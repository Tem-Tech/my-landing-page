about

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is an American media franchise and shared universe centered on a series of superhero films produced by Marvel Studios. The films are based on characters that appear in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.Marvel phases are a way of grouping several films in the MCU together. Phase 1 introduces us to each of the Avengers. Phase 2 handles the fallout from The Avengers. Phase 3 features an uneasiness among the superheroes after Avengers: Age of Ultron (and introduces more heroes), leading us into the showstopping Avengers: Infinity War, and Avengers: Endgame. My Favourite Characters

The Black Panther

King T'Challa- The ruler and protector of Wakanda; a sacred title that must be both inherited & earned. Ceremonially consuming a mystical herb enhanced his strength and agility to near-superhuman levels. Having proven himself worthy in a combat tournament open to all Wakandans, his first loyalty lies with his people

Captain America

Steven Grant Rogers Captain America A.K.A The First Avenger, a World War II veteran and a successful test subject of the Super-Soldier Serum.As Marvel characters go,he's pretty much an all-around good guy. He is up-front, loyal, extremely noble, and dependable. He's even considered 'worthy' by Asgardian standards as he's able to weildl Mjolnir

Thor Odinson

God of Thunder Thor is an Asgardian warrior-prince, a self-proclaimed protector of Earth and founding member of the Avengers (despite being the only Avenger that is not from Earth). Thor's greatest weapon, and closest ally, is Mjolnir, an enchanted hammer forged from Uru metal, which has power over storms and can generate energy blasts known as anti-force
